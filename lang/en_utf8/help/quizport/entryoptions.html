Entry page options

These check boxes enable and disable the display of items on the QuizPort's entry page.

Unit name as title
If checked, the unit name will be displayed as the title of the entry page.

Grading
If checked, the QuizPort's grading information will be displayed on the entry page.

Dates
If checked, the QuizPort's open and close dates will be displayed on the entry page.

Attempts
If checked, a table showing details of a user's previous attempts at this QuizPort will be displayed on the entry page. Attempts that may be resumed will have a resume button displayed in the rightmost column.
