Ignore voids

This setting specifies whether void attempts, i.e. abandoned attempts with a grade of 0%, are ignored or included when students' grades for this QuizPort are calculated.

Yes
Void attempts are ignored during the calculation of grades for this QuizPort.

No
Void attempts are not ignored, that is to say they are included in the calculation of grades for this QuizPort.

Void attempts occur when a student starts a QuizPort, but then does not attempt to answer an questions in any quizzes, before leaving. Such attempts can have a significant impact on the grade when the grading method is Average, First or Last. In such situations, the teacher may decide that it is better to ignore the void attempts when the grade is calculated.
