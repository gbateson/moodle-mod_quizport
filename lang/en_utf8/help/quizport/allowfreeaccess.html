Allow free access

This setting specifies whether or not students are given free access to the quizzes in this QuizPort.

Once students have free access, they can access any quiz in the QuizPort, regardless of the quizzes' pre-conditions and post-conditions.

No
The students will not be given free access to quizzes in the QuizPort. In other words, access to the quizzes will always be controlled by the quizzes' pre-conditions and post-conditions.

Yes: Grades
The students will be given free access to the quizzes in the QuizPort, once they have achieved the required grade

Yes: Attempts
The students will be given free access to the quizzes in the QuizPort, once they have completed the required number of attempts.
